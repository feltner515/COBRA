# EnthalpyAux

!syntax description /AuxKernels/EnthalpyAux

\begin{equation}
h = \dfrac{\rho e + P}{\rho}
\end{equation}

with $h$ the specific enthalpy, $\rho$ the density, $\rho e$ the total energy and
P$ the pressure.
