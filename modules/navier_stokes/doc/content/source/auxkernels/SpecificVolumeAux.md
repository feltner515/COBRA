# SpecificVolumeAux

!syntax description /AuxKernels/SpecificVolumeAux

\begin{equation}
v = \dfrac{\alpha * A}{\rho}
\end{equation}

with $v$ the specific volume, $\alpha$ the volume fraction, $A$ the area and $\rho$ the density.
