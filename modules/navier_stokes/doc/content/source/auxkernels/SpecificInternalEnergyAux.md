# SpecificInternalEnergyAux

!syntax description /AuxKernels/SpecificInternalEnergyAux

\begin{equation}
u = e - \dfrac{||\rho \vec{u}||_{L^2}}{2 \rho}
\end{equation}

with $u$ the specific internal energy, $e$ the specific total energy, and $\rho \vec{u}$ the
fluid momentum.
